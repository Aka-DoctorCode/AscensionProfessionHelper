<h1>V02</h1>
<ul>

    <li><b>Modifier Choice:</b> Choose modifier key for blacklisting in options.</li>
    <li><b>Safety Limits:</b> Destroy blocked when addon frame closed. Warning dialog shows before destroying items.</li>
    <li><b>Smart Highlighting:</b> Red border on items with higher item level than equipped, or usable current items.</li>
    <li><b>Smart Blacklisting:</b> Option to blacklist when attempting to delete from bag. Removed from blacklist after deletion.</li>
    <li><b>UI Improvements:</b> Destroy button uses Ascension Suite style, visible in Blacklist and Options tabs.</li>
